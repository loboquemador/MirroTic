Welcome to MirroTic!

MirroTic reimagines classic TicTacToe with a mirroring challenge. Your moves in one grid reflect onto another, adding strategic depth to the game.

Mirroring: Rules set at the start determine how Xs and Os mirror to another quadrant (x or y axis, or both).

Scoring Points: Score points by forming lines of varying lengths; longer lines mean more points. The player with the highest total wins.
